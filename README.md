## âœ… **Metrics & KPIs**

This section outlines the key metrics and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) visualized and tracked within the Customer Sales & Returns Dashboard. These metrics provide insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and the impact of returns on overall revenue.

| Metric             | Description                                                                 |
|--------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Total Sales** | The sum of the revenue generated from all product sales.                   |
| **Total Returns** | The total number or value of all products that have been returned by customers. |
| **Net Revenue** | The total revenue after deducting the value of returned products (Total Sales - Total Returns). |
| **Return Rate (%)**| The percentage of returns compared to the total number of sales or the total sales value. Calculated as: $\frac{\text{Total Returns}}{\text{Total Sales}} \times 100\%$. |
| **Top/Bottom Products** | Identification of best-selling and worst-selling products based on either total revenue generated or return rate. This helps understand product popularity and potential quality or customer satisfaction issues. |
| **Customer Segments** | Categorization of customers based on their return frequency and average order value. This allows for targeted analysis of different customer behaviors and their impact on sales and returns. |
